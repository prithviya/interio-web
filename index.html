Advantage

Why Choose AAC Blocks

Our Features

Energy Saving

The excellent heat insulation property in AAC blocks allows temperatures to be warm in winters and cool in summers. It will reduce your air conditioning cost therefore energy efficiency is saved.

Sound Insulation

The lightweight and porous structure will reduce the high sound reduction. It is mainly used in the construction of hospitals, industries, schools, etc.

Fire Resistance

The material is non-combustible, so it acts fire-resistant depending upon the thickness of the blocks. It acts as a fire safety material.

Great Finishes

The compressive strength of the block is much stronger and better when compared to brick. It will give a good and great finish.

Eco Friendly

AAC blocks are made from non-toxic ingredients and gives good acoustic performance, fire and flood resistance. So it acts as a eco-friendly building material.

Long Lasting

AAC blocks are made from natural and non-toxic material and during the manufacturing process they will produce less waste, so it will lead to a long life without any damage to the building.
