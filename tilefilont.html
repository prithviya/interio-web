TileFixo NT

UltraTech TILEFIXO NT is ideal for internal thin bed wall & floor and external floor applications of tiles, natural stones, granite & marble. It is recommended to be used over comentitious substrates and for tile-on-tile applications.

Features & Benefits

  • Excellent adhesion to substrates
  • Enhances durability
  • Extended open time allowing more time for tile fixing
  • High slip resistance
  • Single component easy to use
  • Self curing
  • Pre-Soaking of tiles isn't required
