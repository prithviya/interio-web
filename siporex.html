Siporex Block

Siporex AAC Slabs are an ideal replacement for conventional R.C.C slab. These slabs can be used for roof & floor applications in new constructions with R.C.C or steel framing system.

Features & Benefits

  • Light Weight.
  • High Thermal Insulation.
  • Fire Resistance.
  • Low Maintenence.
  • Environmentally friendly.
  • Energy Efficient.
  • Fixing.
  • Precision.

    • Technical Specification

    Density(oven dry) 451 - 550 & 551 - 650 kg/m3
    Compressive strength As per IS 2185 Part (III)2
    Thermal Conductivity 0.16 W/M deg. K(Average)
    Resistant to Fire 2-6 hrs.Depending upon ThK
    Sound Reduction Index 37-49dB depending upon Thk
